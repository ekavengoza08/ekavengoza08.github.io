# THE TALLEST BUILDING IN THE WORLD
![image](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/9/93/Burj_Khalifa.jpg/1200px-Burj_Khalifa.jpg)
Burj Khalifa is located in Dubai, the tallest building in the world, succeeding the Taipei 101 in Taipei, Taiwan. The building was inaugurated on January 04, 2010. The building is an 828 meters tall and it has attracted visitors since it has opened. The Burj Khalifa has the highest number of stories in the world and has the highest outdoor observation deck in the world. It also has the elevator with the longest travel distance in the world. The building was built and designed by a Chicago-based architectural, urban planning, and engineering firm Skidmore, Owings and Merrill (SOM).
